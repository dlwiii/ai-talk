HOW WE USE AI

AI in Meetings

  • Let it record and transcribe — be present, stop scribbling notes
  • Have it pre-load context before, and write the summary + follow-ups after
  • Give it access to your email, calendar & Slack → it grasps the full picture
  • Ask it later to recall anything — a number, a decision, who said what
  • Honest caveat: works far better in Google Meet than MS Teams
